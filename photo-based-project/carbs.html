Carbs

Carbohydrates, often called "carbs," are like the body's primary fuel. They provide quick and vital energy for our daily activities. Carbs are found in foods like bread, pasta, and rice. But they do more than just give us energy; they help our brains work efficiently. In fact, the brain's favorite fuel is glucose, which comes from carbs.
