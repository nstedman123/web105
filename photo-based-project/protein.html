Protein

Protein is like the body's building blocks. It's crucial because it helps grow and repair tissues, like muscles and skin. Our hair and nails are mostly made of protein, too. Protein also plays a role in making important chemicals, like enzymes and hormones, which help our bodies function properly. It's essential for our immune system to fight off illnesses and diseases.
