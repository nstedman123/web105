Fiber

Fiber, though often overlooked, plays a vital role in keeping our bodies healthy. It's like the broom that sweeps away waste and toxins, helping our digestive system work smoothly. Fiber is found in foods like fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. It adds bulk to our meals, making us feel full and satisfied.
